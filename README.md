# ☕ BrewMetrics Cafe Dashhboard

---

## 📌 Problem

BrewMetrics Cafe lacked a centralized view of sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends. Key business questions such as identifying top-performing products, understanding customer retention, and analyzing revenue channels were difficult to answer efficiently.

---

## 💡 Solution

Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze 30K orders and ₹17.7M revenue.

The dashboard provides insights into:

* Revenue trends and Month-over-Month growth.
* Product and category performance.
* Customer segmentation and repeat customer analysis.
* Revenue contribution by online and in-store channels.
* Sales patterns by time of day.
* Key KPIs including AOV, quantity sold, and customer retention.

---

## 📈 Key Findings

* Hot and Cold Coffee categories contributed the highest revenue.
* Java Chip Frappuccino was the top-performing product.
* Online and in-store revenue were almost equally distributed.
* Repeat customers accounted for 57% of customer activity.
* Revenue recovered strongly after a temporary dip in February.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* Power BI
* Excel
* DAX
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization

---

## 🚀 Summary

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales, customer behavior, and product performance, enabling data-driven decision-making and helping identify opportunities for growth and customer retention.
